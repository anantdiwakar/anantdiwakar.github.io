About me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Aerospace Engineering, IIT Bombay, working under the supervision of Prof. Kowsik Bodi.

I am interested in modeling and numerical simulations of complex flows like flows at high temperatures under the influence of electric and magnetic fields.
These types of flow are typically encountered in arc heated tunnels and during atmospheric reentry of spacecrafts.
